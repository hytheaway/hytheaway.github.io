who are you?
i am a graduate student in new york studying audio... stuff. my graduate work focuses on methods to improve binaural externalization without the use of individualized head-related transfer functions.

as of writing (in may 2024), i am 23 years old, which places the date of my birth circa 2000 - 2001.

what is hytheaway supposed to mean?
it's a pun between the arcaic word "hythe" and the not-so-archaic word "hideaway".
so pronounce it the same way you would pronounce "hideaway", just with a "th" sound instead of a "d".
